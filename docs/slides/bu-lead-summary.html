Agentic Apps — Vibe Coding Workshop
Program Summary for BU Leads
The Play
A hands-on 2-hour lab where a customer technical team builds a production-grade AI agent end-to-end on Databricks. Participants use vibe coding (Claude + Coda) to build, deploy, evaluate, and harden an agent — touching Apps, Lakebase, and MLflow evals in one session. No setup required from the customer: SA runs workspace prep in advance.
Why It Matters for Your Team
Moves deals from scoping to active POC. Customers leave with a working app in their own workspace, quantified eval scores, and a clear hardening path. Drives Apps + Lakebase + Model Serving co-adoption in a single motion. Sets POC status to Active in Salesforce.
What Your Team Needs to Do
AE: identify accounts in U2/U3 with a defined AI use case and a technical team available for a half-day session. File ASQ — Overlay SA option. SA/DSA: run workspace setup 1 week ahead, deliver the lab, capture use cases and eval scores as leave-behinds.
Targets and Success Signals
75 sessions / 150 apps built (FY27 field target). Leading indicators: Apps created, Lakebase instances provisioned, MLflow eval runs. Trailing: AI DBU consumption lift 60 days post-session, POC-to-close rate. File ASQ for every delivery.
